Privacy Policy

This is the Privacy Policy page for NatureFacts

1. Introduction

Welcome to NatureFacts! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number.
  • Delivery Information: Shipping address and payment details.
  • Browsing Data: IP address, browser information, and cookies.
  • Purchase History: Details about your orders and preferences.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To enhance the functionality of our website and your shopping experience.
  • To inform you about our products, promotions, and updates (with your consent).
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to improve website functionality, analyze traffic, and understand user preferences. You can adjust cookie settings through your browser at any time.

5. How We Protect Your Data

We take appropriate measures to secure your information against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. This includes using advanced encryption technology and implementing strict security protocols.

6. Sharing Your Information with Third Parties

We do not share your personal data with third parties except in the following cases:

  • Delivery Services: To fulfill your orders.
  • Payment Processors: To securely process payments.
  • Government Authorities: When required by law.

All third-party services we work with are obligated to protect your data and use it only as necessary for our collaboration.

7. Your Rights

  • Access the information we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Withdraw your consent for data processing.
  • File a complaint with data protection authorities if you believe your rights have been violated.

To exercise your rights, please contact us using the details provided below.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it regularly.
